Alfredo Sauce

A delightful helping of the best food in the whole world!

Fettucine Alfredo and specifically Alfredo Sauce is by far some of the best food I have ever put in my mouth. It is delicate and quite soft on the mouth.

Here as follows is my personal favorite recipe. I make it quite often.

Ingredients for the sauce

  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese
Steps to delish
  1. Melt full stick of butter over Medium heat. Add garlic and saute for 1 minute. Do not to burn garlic.
  2. Add in heavy whipping cream and stir.
  3. Lastly add in your grated Parmesan cheese.

    4. Enjoy; Serve atop your favorite noodle of choice.

    Some good recommendations are Penne, Fettucine, and Cavatappi.
