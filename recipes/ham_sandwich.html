Ham Sandwich aka The Ham Sammy

An amazing piece of history is about to be made.

Prepare yourself for this Ham Sammy is nothing to mess around with! Best prepared fresh.

Here as follows is my personal favorite recipe. I make it quite often.

Ingredients for the sammich

  • 2 pieces of your choice of bread
  • 4 thin slices of ham
  • Pickles!!!
  • 2 slices of muenster cheese
  • Mayo
Steps to delish
  1. Grab your 2 pieces of bread and slather on mayo. Dont worry about overdoing it as that is very hard.
  2. Tear your cheese into little bits and then put on top piece of bread. Sprinkle heavily across.
  3. Add your pickles next, the more the better.
  4. Finally, add your sliced ham. Put bottom piece of bread on.

    5. Enjoy; Serve with potato chips or beans. BEANS!

    Another good recommendation is putting sandwich in microwave and letting the cheese melt.
